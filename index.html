Felix

Canis lupus familiaris
Schnauzer and Poodle stock

Favorites include

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Head scratches
  • New people

Not a fan of...

  • Toddlers
  • Dog food
  • Loud noises
  • Sunlight

Quiet, kind, and playful - always ready to chase but never brings the ball back. Not only does he let the toddler pull his ears and tail without even a turn of his head, he "protects" him when Papa is roughhousing with them! In my experience, Schnoodles are the most family-friendly dog on the planet.
